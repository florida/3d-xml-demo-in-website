Class A Office Building


Features

Situated at the foot of Millennium Park, the 83-story Aon Center offers spectacular views of the City and Lake Michigan from even the lowest floors. The building boasts a first class amenity package including a 243-seat auditorium with audio-visual system, heated, 800-car parking garage, the newly remodeled Mid-America Club on the 80th floor and direct access to a 4-star hotel. Tenants also enjoy the convenience of an on-site post office, coffee shop, cafes, banking, barber shop and florist. The lower-level plaza contains a fascinating reflecting pool with outdoor seating.
